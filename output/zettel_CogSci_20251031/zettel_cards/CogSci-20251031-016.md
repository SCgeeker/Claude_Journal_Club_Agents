# LLMs in Psycholinguistic Research

> **核心**: "The current chapter provides an overview of their application in psycholinguistic research, that is, the employment of LLMs for the purpose of studying human language behavior and language processing."

## 說明
此研究聚焦 LLM 在心理語言學中的多元應用，包括驚奇度測量、嵌入抽取與提示調查。此外，也探討其在語法、語義、語篇層面的可對應性與局限。

# Limitation of MLMs for Online Comprehension

> **核心**: "Using a MLM to study online sentence comprehension is unwarranted given that the model expects information from (not yet presented) subsequent context."

## 說明
MLM 需要未來上下文才能預測缺失詞，因而不適用於研究即時閱讀或句子即時理解。此限制凸顯了雙向模型與人類即時處理的差異。
